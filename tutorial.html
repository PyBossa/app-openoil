Tutorial

Hi!

Thanks for collaborating in this oil contracts crowdcrafting project. By transcribing the FULL LEGAL NAME and JURISDICTION of each CONTRACTOR of the following contracts, you will help create corporate network maps of the world's first comprehensive archive of published oil contracts.

The process is very easy. Simply search through the following documents and fill in the form. For more information, please follow this tutorial."

The first one is "Full legal name"

Search through the contract and fill in the names of each contractor, one by one. In order to identify each company, it is imported to write the FULL LEGAL NAME, i.e. "Septa Energy Nigeria Limited" rather than simply "Septa".

The second one is "Jurisdiction"

It is common in oil contracts to mention the jurisdiction of each contractor, i.e. “Septa Energy Nigeria Limited“, “incorporated under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria“. If this is the case, please only fill in "Nigeria".

Finally, the "OpenCorporates Identifier"

Please open up: www.opencorporates.com and see if you can find the company there. As there can be multiple companies with very similar names, please make sure that legal name and jurisdiction really match an entry on OpenCorporates, before you fill it in. Generally, OpenCorporates ID's look something like this: gb/00102498 and can be found in the URL of a company page.

There may be one or more contractors, so you can add new ones or remove them by clicking on or buttons.

And that's all! If you need a reminder on what exactly should be on each field, please push on the "Help" button you'll be able to see these instructions again. Thank you!
