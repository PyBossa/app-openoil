Tutorial

Hi! short welcome and explanation of what this is

To help us, you'll have to find the contractors within the 5-page PDF files you'll be presented.

There may be one or more, so you can add or remove new contractors by clicking on or buttons.

The first one is "Full legal name"

Search through the contract and fill in the names of each contractor, one by one. In order to identify each company, it is imported to write the FULL LEGAL NAME, i.e. "Shell Nigeria Developement Company" rather than simply "Shell".

The second one is "Jurisdiction"

It is common in oil contracts to mention the jurisdiction of each contractor, i.e. "BP International Limited, registered in the United Kingdom". If this is the case, please only fill in "United Kingdom".

Finally, the "Jurisdiction"

Please open up: www.opencorporates.com and see if you can find the company there. As there can be multiple companies with very similar names, please make sure that legal name and jurisdiction really match an entry on OpenCorporates, before you fill it in. Generally, OpenCorporates ID's look something like this: gb/00102498 and can be found in the URL of a company page.

And that's all! If you need a reminder on what exactly should be on each field, please push on the "Help" button you'll find to see these instructions again. Thank you!
